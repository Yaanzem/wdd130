Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an online presence for Plateau the Beautiful Outdoor Rafting. The site aims to attract potential customers, provide information about available trips, and facilitate bookings and inquiries.

Audience

The target audience for this website includes adventure enthusiasts, families looking for outdoor activities, and corporate groups seeking team-building experiences. Three scenario questions:
1. Are you looking for a thrilling outdoor adventure?
2. Are you planning a family trip and want a memorable experience?
3. Are you organizing a corporate team-building event?
